Introduction

2020 was the year of DEFI , Uniswap and Ethereum. Hundred's of innovative Decentralised Financial products launched on Ethereum and with that brought new words into the crypto vernacular- liquidity pools, rugs and DEX.

The pace of innovation is very quick in DEFI and solutions to problems around token sales, liquidity and locking of liquidity came quick and fast. But these were all focused upon Ethereum and Uniswap.

2021 saw gas fees on the Ethereum network become so expensive that projects were forced to seek alternative chain solutions to launch or cross chain their platforms. The blockchain to benefit the most was Binance Smart Chain (BSC). BSC and it's Uniswap competitor, Pancake Swap have seen huge increases in volume but the solutions to provide a safer environment for investors to inject funds into projects has yet to follow.

That is why we have built The Fridge. A BSC token sale platform that automatically adds liquidity on Pancake Swap and locks that liquidity through it's smart contracts.

More trust, more security, safer investments.

A Platform For Projects and Investors

As the DEFI environment has suffered from various bad actors obtaining investor funds illicitly, legitimate projects have suffered as a result of investor paranoia. Project owners can now choose to carry out their token sales on The Fridge and reassure investors that raised funds will be automatically locked as liquidity.

Investors can also invest into The Fridge token sales, safe in the knowledge that their purchased tokens will have the designated liquidity and therefore a tradable market for an extended period, this is one less thing to worry about in the investment decision.

Fridge charges 2% of BNB raised on the token sale platform as long as softcap is met. 60% of this is available through our staking pool for token holders. For example, if a project raised 3000 BNB, Fridge would earn 60 BNB. 36 BNB would be added into the staking pools to be earned by stakers.

The Fridge will also charge 1% of the native token raise. For example, if a project is selling 100,000 of their native token on the Fridge sales platform, an additional 1000 tokens would be transferred to the Fridge deployer wallet.
